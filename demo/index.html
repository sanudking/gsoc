Interactive Physics Playground

Drag the objects with your mouse! Watch the pendulum swing and the shapes slide down the ramps.

Documented Physics Parameters:

  • Global Gravity: y = 1 (Standard downward force).
  • Pendulum System: Bob Restitution (Bounciness) = 0.5, String Stiffness = 0.9.
  • Ramp 1 (Steep - Left): Angle = 45°, Static Friction = High. The blue circle has rolling friction.
  • Ramp 2 (Shallow - Right): Angle = ~22.5°, Static Friction = High. The purple box has sliding friction = 0.05.
